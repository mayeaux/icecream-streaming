Welcome to icecream the live video broadcaster.

Get started now

With icecream you can live stream your workshops, key note speakers, personal performances or anything you can capture on your web camera.

Use any of your May First/People Link username/passwords to start streaming. Your stream will be identified by your username, so you may want to create a username just for streaming.

All streams are public and recorded - with recordings available for download for 24 hours before they are removed.

So what are you waiting for? Fix your hair and start streaming!
